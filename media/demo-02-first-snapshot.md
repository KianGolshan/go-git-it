## Step 2 — Take your first snapshot

---

You've written some code. Maybe you set up the HTML structure, or finished the navigation bar, or got the homepage looking right. **Now save it.**

Click **📸 Snapshot** in the sidebar.

A prompt asks: *What did you just work on?* This is optional but useful — something like:

> `Set up homepage layout`

> `Added the nav bar`

> `Got the hero section working`

Go Git It saves **all your changed files** as a single snapshot with your description and a timestamp.

---

### What you'll see

The status line at the top of the sidebar changes to:

> 🔵 Saved here, not on GitHub yet

And a new dot appears at the top of your timeline — blue, because it's saved on your computer but not yet sent to GitHub.

---

### When should I snapshot?

Think of it like saving a game:
- ✅ After finishing a feature or section
- ✅ Before trying something you're not sure about
- ✅ At the end of every work session
- ✅ Whenever the thing you just built is *working*

There's no such thing as too many snapshots.

---
