## Step 6 — Work inside the experiment

---

You're now in your experiment. Go wild. Delete things, rewrite things, try things you'd never do on the main line.

**Snapshot inside the experiment exactly like you normally would** — click 📸 Snapshot, describe what you did.

---

### Example: Hero redesign experiment

| What you did | Snapshot |
|---|---|
| Deleted the old hero HTML | `Wiped old hero` |
| Built new layout structure | `New hero skeleton` |
| Added the background gradient | `Gradient background` |
| Got the animation working | `Scroll animation done` |

These 4 snapshots only exist inside the experiment. Your main line still has the original hero — completely unchanged.

---

### Switching back to check your main line

If you want to peek at what your main line looks like:

1. Click **🗑️ Abandon experiment** ← don't worry, you can start a new one
2. ...or just check the timeline — the dots in the experiment section are teal 🔵, not green

Actually, the easiest way to check is just to look at your files — while you're in the experiment, the files reflect the experiment. After merging or abandoning, they reflect main.

---

### Can I have multiple experiments?

Not at the same time — you can only be on one branch at once. Finish or abandon the current experiment before starting a new one.

---

### When are you ready to decide?

When you've made enough progress to know whether the experiment is worth keeping. You don't have to finish everything — you can merge a work-in-progress experiment too.
